Recruit town NPCs and boss companions to fight alongside you! Equip humanoid companions with gear, unlock boss companions through quests, and bring the fight to Terraria with a loyal ally at your side.

Features:
- Recruit town NPCs as combat companions
- Unlock boss companions through quest chains
- Equip humanoid companions with weapons, armor, and ammo
- Per-companion equipment restrictions and themed combat kits
- Companion stat scaling with world progression
- Full multiplayer support
- API for other mods to register custom companions and quests
